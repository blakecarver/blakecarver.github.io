The Sinner's Lot

The Sinner's Lot is a southern gothic tale set in the fictional town of Ashfield. The story follows Gatlin Harrington, the unwanted son of Sutton Harrington, who dies by suicide shortly after his birth. Gatlin is left in the care of his aunt, Helene, a deeply religious woman whose faith offers little comfort to a child ostracized by their small community.

Gatlin's very existence is a dark secret. He is the result of a brutal assault on his mother, an event that left a once vivacious young woman with a broken spirit. Sutton held on just long enough to give birth, then took her own life, hanging herself from an oak tree in the backyard. As Gatlin grows up, he is a constant reminder of the tragedy that befell his mother and the shame of his unknown father. Shunned by his own family and the townspeople, he must navigate a life burdened by a legacy he never asked for.

The Sinner's Lot is currently in its early draft stages, and a release date has not yet been set. For exclusive updates and to read excerpts as they become available, you can subscribe to my Patreon.
